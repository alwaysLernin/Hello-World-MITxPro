Relax and enjoy the ride.

A long time ago, life was stressful because we had to worry about things like getting eaten by a sabertooth tiger. Back then, when the tiger was gone, we could relax a little.

Today, we don't have to worry about that tiger, but it seems that we've managed to find an infinite number of modern day tigers that keep us stressed all day long.

We all need to practice putting these new tigers away and relax.



Things that can help us to relax:

  • Take a walk
  • Go find a body of water
    • A pool
    • A lake
    • The ocean
    • A fish tank - YouTube - Aquarium
  • Listen to relaxing music. For example - YouTube - Relaxing water sounds
  • Talk to a friend or loved one
  • Start a new hobby
